Post-Order Patent Owner Petitions

Patent owner petitions to the Office of Petitions (document code PET.OP or RXPET.) filed after ex parte reexamination was ordered — e.g., requests to reconsider the grant or terminate the proceeding — with any requester opposition and the Office's decision.

After a reexamination is ordered (a substantial new question of patentability was found), a patent owner may petition the Office of Petitions to reconsider that grant or terminate the proceeding. Where a proceeding has multiple PET.OP documents (the petition plus exhibits), the shortest is treated as the petition itself. The requester opposition (RXOPPPET or RXPET.) and the Office's petition decision are shown where on file.
