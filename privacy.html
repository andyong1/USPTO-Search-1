Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 7, 2026

This is a free, non-commercial tool for searching publicly available U.S. patent and reexamination data from the USPTO Open Data Portal, for monitoring specific applications, and for receiving optional email alerts about ex parte reexamination determinations. It displays no advertising and is not operated for profit. This policy explains what limited information the site handles.

1. No accounts, no tracking

  • There are no user accounts and no logins — you don't create credentials or provide identity to use the site.
  • The site uses no advertising, no analytics, and no tracking or marketing cookies. We do not build profiles of visitors or track you across other sites.

2. Information we store

We do not automatically collect personal information from visitors. The only information stored is what you voluntarily provide through two optional features:

(a) Tracked Proceedings. When you add an application to monitor, we store:

  • the patent application number(s) you choose to monitor,
  • an optional label you assign, and
  • the email address(es) you enter to receive new-filing alerts.

(b) Reexamination determination email alerts. When you subscribe on the Reexamination Determinations page, we store your email address (and a private token used only to generate your unsubscribe link). This is used solely to send you a once-daily email listing ex parte reexamination determinations issued the prior day; no email is sent on days with none.

This information is stored only to operate these features. We do not sell, rent, or share it, and we do not use these email addresses for any purpose other than the alerts you requested.

You can stop and remove your data at any time: delete a tracked application using the controls on the page, or unsubscribe from reexamination alerts using the one-click unsubscribe link included in every alert email (which deletes your email address from the list).

3. Searches

When you run a search or look up an application, your query is sent to the USPTO Open Data Portal API to return results. The site does not retain a history of your individual searches. Patent information displayed by the site is public record maintained by the USPTO.

4. Service providers

The site relies on a few third-party services to function. Your use of the site is also subject to their respective privacy practices:

  • USPTO Open Data Portal (data.uspto.gov) — source of all patent data; search requests are sent here.
  • Vercel — website hosting. As the host, Vercel may automatically log standard technical request data (such as IP address, timestamp, and browser information) for security and operations, per Vercel's own privacy policy.
  • Neon / Vercel Postgres — database that stores the Tracked Proceedings data described above.
  • Resend — email delivery service used to send the tracked-proceeding and reexamination alert emails to the addresses you provide.

We do not control, and are not responsible for, the independent data practices of these providers.

5. Data security and retention

Stored data is retained until you remove it — by deleting the corresponding tracked application, or by unsubscribing from reexamination alerts — or until it is otherwise removed. We take reasonable measures to protect the limited information stored, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" above.
